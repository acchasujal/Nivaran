# CivicPulse: Product Roadmap & Metrics

This document defines the feature hierarchy, North Star metrics, strategic recommendations, and 2032 long-term vision for CivicPulse.

---

## 1. Feature Hierarchy

### Must Have
*   **Evidence Submission & Validation:** deterministic checks and image verification.
*   **Deduplication & Clustering:** spatial grouping of nearby reports.
*   **Chronological Detail View:** a single scrollable case timeline.
*   **Human Approval Gate:** draft approval mechanism for legal notices.
*   **Citizen Tracking Workspace:** device-saved tracking links without forced login.
*   **Repair Verification:** submission of after-photos to verify government closure.
*   **Accessibility Mode:** high-contrast and keyboard-accessible list fallbacks.

### Should Have
*   **WhatsApp & Voice Intake:** lightweight reporting channels.
*   **Multilingual plain-language alerts:** in Hindi, Marathi, Tamil, Telugu, and Kannada.
*   **RTI Generation:** automated, editable legal drafting templates.
*   **Government reply channel integration:** replying directly via email/SMS.
*   **NGO/RWA attribution tags:** simple organization verification badges.

### Nice to Have
*   **Ward-level aggregate analytics** behind authenticated dashboards.
*   **Offline draft queuing** with local device storage.
*   **Exportable evidence dossiers** for community advocates.

---

## 2. North Star Metrics

Our metrics evaluate actual resolution and trust, not performative platform usage:

*   **Primary Metric:** *Verified Civic Resolution Rate* — the percentage of cases that reach independently verified resolution within DARPG/RTI SLAs.
*   **Supporting Metrics:**
    *   Time from submission to validated case.
    *   Percentage of reports correctly grouped into clusters.
    *   Government acknowledgement rate and median response time.
    *   Percentage of AI drafts edited/rejected by citizens (assessing AI drafting utility).
    *   Accessibility flow completion rates.
    *   Reputation and fraud flag detection rates.
*   **Metrics to Avoid Optimizing:** Raw report volumes, number of escalations, page views, and public outrage metrics.

---

## 3. Product Roadmap & 2032 Vision

### The Citizen Experience
By 2032, reporting is omnichannel (WhatsApp, voice, SMS, in-person kiosk). Language, digital literacy, and cellular connectivity are no longer barriers. AI operates invisibly to format, classify, and translate submissions, while citizens track cases via simple plain-language timelines.

### The Government Experience
Municipalities integrate CivicPulse as an evidence-normalization layer. Officers update and resolve cases directly from their email or WhatsApp. Commissioners analyze ward trends backed by audit-trail-verified data.

### The NGO Experience
NGOs manage local issue portfolios, coordinate neighborhood verification drives, and output evidence-backed dossiers to advocate for systemic infrastructure improvements.
