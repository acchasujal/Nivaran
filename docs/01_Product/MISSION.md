# CivicPulse: Product Mission

This document defines the core mission, target audience promises, and fundamental user values of CivicPulse.

---

## 1. The Mission

> **Make credible civic evidence impossible to disappear.**

We achieve this by anchoring raw photo/voice evidence into a public community ledger, ensuring that no report vanishes into administrative queues without public accountability.

---

## 2. Core User Value

> **“CivicPulse tells me what happened to my evidence, what I can do next, and whether the public problem actually improved.”**

---

## 3. Product Promises

### To Citizens
> Your evidence will be explained, protected, joined with relevant community evidence, and given a visible path toward action.

### To Governments
> You will receive structured, deduplicated, location-specific cases with clear evidence, impact metrics, and citizen expectations.

### To NGOs and Communities
> You can organize credible local evidence into collective cases without losing attribution, ownership, or trust.

---

## 4. Summary Identity

*   **One Sentence:** CivicPulse turns verified local evidence into collective, trackable civic action.
*   **One Word:** Accountability.
