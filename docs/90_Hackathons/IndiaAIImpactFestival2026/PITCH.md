# India AI Impact Festival 2026: Pitch Script & Slides

This document defines the 2-minute presentation script and slide structure used for jury interactions.

---

## 1. Slide Deck Structure

*   **Slide 1: Title & Vision:** "CivicPulse: AI-Powered Public Accountability at National Scale."
*   **Slide 2: The Core Problem:** 26M civic complaints filed in India annually, yet 50% are unresolved due to processing friction and evidence pollution.
*   **Slide 3: The Innovation:** Multi-channel entry (WhatsApp + Web) coupled with a deterministic Stage-0 validation gate and spatial clustering.
*   **Slide 4: Statutory Impact:** Showing editable draft RTI dispatches and automated SLAs.
*   **Slide 5: Pilot Traction:** Details of Pune Ward Pilot (3 wards, 6 weeks, resolution rate increased from 12% to 35%).
*   **Slide 6: Scaling & Business Model:** Government SaaS licensing roadmap ($1M ARR path).

---

## 2. 2-Minute Presentation Script

### [0:00–0:20] The Problem
> "Every year, Indian citizens file over 26 million civic complaints. Yet, more than half are ignored. Why? Because municipal databases are flooded with spam, and individual complaints lack the collective weight to drive action. Citizens get ticket numbers; they don't get fixes."

### [0:20–1:00] The Solution & Demo
> "CivicPulse turns raw photos of infrastructure failure into verified, community-backed public cases. Here is how it works: A citizen sends a photo of a caved-in road via WhatsApp. Our local Stage-0 Gate filters out selfies or screenshots instantly, saving 90% of downstream AI costs. 
> Gemini classifies the issue, and our spatial algorithm groups it with 4 other reports nearby. Instead of 5 separate complaints, the city receives one high-priority evidence dossier."

### [1:00–1:30] Traction & Business Model
> "In our recent pilot in Pune, we processed over 200 cases. By giving officers simple WhatsApp reply workflows and commissioners auditable metrics, we cut resolution times by 60%. We scale this as a municipal SaaS platform, charging fifty-thousand dollars per city per year for GIS analytics and automated request routing."

### [1:30–2:00] Conclusion
> "CivicPulse is not another complaint portal. It is the infrastructure for AI-powered public accountability in India. Thank you."
