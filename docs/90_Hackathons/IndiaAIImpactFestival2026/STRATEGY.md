## 2. Competitive Differentiators

*   **Evidence Chain of Custody:** Unlike systems that rely on public upvotes (which can be gamed), CivicPulse uses deterministic photo validation, spatial clustering, and verified status updates.
*   **Government-Centric Integrations:** Rather than building citizen-only shaming portals, CivicPulse designs workflows for officers (WhatsApp/email integration) and commissioners (data audits).
*   **Statutory RTI Power:** Generates official Section 6(1) Right to Information drafts, giving citizens legal recourse.

---

## 3. Business & Sustainability Model

*   **Revenue Source:** Municipal SaaS licensing fee ($50,000 / city / year) providing automated request sorting and GIS analytics dashboard access.
*   **NGO Integration:** Muted tier offering bulk export dossiers to coordinate community groups.
